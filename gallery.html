Community Healthcare Initiatives

We conduct free dermatology consultation and awareness camps in and around Pattukkottai for the benefit of the poor and needy people

Free Camp Highlights:

  • Free consultation for common skin, hair, and nail issues
  • On-the-spot diagnosis using advanced dermatoscopy
  • Patient education on seasonal skin care and treatment options
  • Emphasis on awareness of chronic dermatological conditions
  • Distribution of free medicenes

These initiatives reflect Our dedication for making quality skin care accessible to all, bridging gaps in rural and semi-urban healthcare delivery.
