Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a prior appointment to visit the clinic?

Walk-ins are welcome, but prior appointments are preferred to avoid waiting.

Is the consultation suitable for both men and women?

Absolutely! Treatments are available for all age groups, skin types, and genders.

Are all the products used in treatment dermatologically approved?

Yes. We use only high-quality, dermatologist-tested products and protocols.

Can I get a virtual consultation?

Yes. Online consultations are available for patients outside Pattukkottai, those staying abroad or those in travel.

Is the treatment safe for sensitive skin or during pregnancy?

All treatments are personalized and safety-checked. Dr. Pradeepa ensures treatments are safe for all skin types, including during pregnancy.
