Treatment FAQs

How many sessions will I need for my treatment?

The number of sessions varies depending on the treatment and your individual condition. During your consultation, Dr. Pradeepa will assess your needs and provide a personalized treatment plan with the estimated number of sessions required.

Is there any downtime after treatments?

Most treatments have minimal to no downtime. Some procedures like chemical peels or laser treatments may require a short recovery period where you might experience temporary redness or sensitivity. Dr. Pradeepa will provide detailed aftercare instructions specific to your treatment.

Are the treatments painful?

We use advanced techniques and technologies to minimize discomfort during treatments. Most patients report only mild sensations during procedures. For more sensitive treatments, we can use topical anesthetics to ensure your comfort.

How long do the results last?

The longevity of results depends on the specific treatment and individual factors like skin type, age, and lifestyle. Some treatments provide permanent results (like tattoo removal), while others may require maintenance sessions (like laser hair reduction). Dr. Pradeepa will discuss expected outcomes during your consultation.
